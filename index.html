Warehouse Management Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check the inventory of a specific part?

Navigate to the "Cars" section in the main menu, select the vehicle model, then choose the part category. You'll see all available parts with their current stock levels.

What does the low stock alert mean?

When stock levels fall below 5 units for any part, the system automatically highlights it in red and displays an alert. This indicates you should reorder the part soon.

How can I find a part's location in the warehouse?

Each part in the system shows its exact location (Rack and Shelf number). You can also use the search function to quickly locate parts by name or part number.

How do I update inventory counts?

Use the + and - buttons next to each part's quantity to adjust stock levels. Changes are saved automatically in real-time.

Where can I view warehouse performance metrics?

The "Analytics" section provides detailed reports on inventory turnover, retrieval times, and workforce productivity.
